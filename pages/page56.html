Quantum Entanglement: New Discoveries in Particle Physics

Published in Nature | Vol. 625 | January 2025

Nature journal publishes groundbreaking research on quantum entanglement phenomena. Scientists at CERN demonstrate unprecedented control over entangled particle states, opening new possibilities for quantum computing and secure communications.

The research team successfully maintained quantum entanglement over unprecedented distances, demonstrating coherence times exceeding 10 milliseconds. This breakthrough addresses major obstacles in quantum information processing and establishes new protocols for quantum teleportation experiments.
