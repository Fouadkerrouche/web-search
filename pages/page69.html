Comprehensive Review of Blockchain Scalability Approaches

ACM Computing Surveys | Vol. 57, No. 3

ACM Computing Surveys presents exhaustive analysis of blockchain scaling solutions. Research evaluates sharding, layer-2 protocols, and alternative consensus mechanisms, comparing transaction throughput, security guarantees, and decentralization trade-offs across 50+ implementations.

Empirical testing reveals state channels and optimistic rollups achieve 100,000+ transactions per second while preserving security. Comparative analysis identifies optimal architectures for different use cases, from financial applications requiring immediate finality to supply chain systems prioritizing throughput over latency.
