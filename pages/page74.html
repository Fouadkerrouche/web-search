Cooperative Multi-Agent Systems for Complex Task Coordination

Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research | Vol. 79

Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research introduces novel framework for multi-agent reinforcement learning. Algorithm enables autonomous robots to dynamically form coalitions and coordinate complex tasks without centralized control, demonstrating applications in warehouse automation and disaster response.

Decentralized learning with communication protocols achieves 94% efficiency in collaborative tasks. Agents develop emergent strategies through shared reward structures, outperforming centralized approaches by 35% in dynamic environments. Real-world deployment in Amazon warehouses reduces package sorting time by 40%.
