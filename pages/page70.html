Global Pathways to Carbon Neutrality by 2050

Nature Climate Change | Vol. 15, No. 1

Nature Climate Change analyzes feasible scenarios for achieving net-zero emissions. Study models energy system transformations across 195 countries, identifying optimal combinations of renewable sources, energy storage, and policy interventions required for climate targets.

Integration of 85% renewable electricity by 2040 requires $4.5 trillion annual investment but generates net economic benefits through reduced fossil fuel dependence. Advanced battery storage and green hydrogen enable grid stability, while carbon pricing mechanisms accelerate transition.
