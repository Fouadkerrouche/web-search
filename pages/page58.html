Long-term Efficacy of mRNA Vaccines: Clinical Trial Results

The New England Journal of Medicine | December 2024

The New England Journal of Medicine publishes comprehensive data on mRNA vaccine performance over 3-year period. Study includes 50,000 participants across multiple demographics, showing sustained immune response and minimal adverse effects.

Results demonstrate 89% effectiveness against severe disease after three years, with antibody levels remaining stable beyond initial projections. The research validates mRNA technology as a platform for future vaccine development across multiple infectious diseases.
