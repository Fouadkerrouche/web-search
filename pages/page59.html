Novel Approaches to Cardiovascular Disease Prevention

The Lancet | Vol. 404, No. 10450

The Lancet presents groundbreaking research on early intervention strategies for heart disease. International collaborative study demonstrates 40% reduction in cardiac events through lifestyle modifications and targeted therapies.

The multi-center trial followed 75,000 participants over five years, implementing personalized prevention programs based on genetic risk factors and biomarker profiles. Integration of AI-powered risk assessment tools enabled early identification of high-risk individuals, facilitating timely interventions.
