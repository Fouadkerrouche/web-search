Personalized Immunotherapy Doubles Survival Rates in Advanced Cancer

Journal of Clinical Oncology | Vol. 43, No. 2

Journal of Clinical Oncology reports breakthrough results from Phase III clinical trial. Personalized CAR-T cell therapy combined with checkpoint inhibitors achieves 72% response rate in previously untreatable solid tumors, transforming cancer treatment paradigm.

Tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte analysis guides T-cell engineering for patient-specific antigens. Five-year survival improves from 15% to 42% in metastatic melanoma, with complete response observed in 28% of participants. Minimal side effects compared to conventional chemotherapy enhance quality of life.
